Splashanyone Waterpark

Our waterpark is the biggest and best park in the entire western world. Just south of Interstate 2015, you’ll find a sweet mix of super slides.

If that wasn’t enough, we have 376 of the best food trucks in the state serving everything from fried tummy bears to lemonade and pizza.

But we wouldn’t be Splashanyone without the rules about splashing…wait a minute, there are no rules.
